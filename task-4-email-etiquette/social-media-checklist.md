✅ Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Do’s
- ✅ Think before posting — ensure your content is respectful and professional.
- ✅ Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal details.
- ✅ Use social media to build a positive academic and professional image.
- ✅ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- ✅ Engage constructively in discussions and respect differing opinions.
Don’ts
- ❌ Don’t post offensive or discriminatory content.
- ❌ Don’t overshare sensitive personal information (location, financial details, etc.).
- ❌ Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative online behavior.
- ❌ Don’t plagiarize or share academic work without proper credit.
- ❌ Don’t let excessive social media use interfere with studies or responsibilities.
