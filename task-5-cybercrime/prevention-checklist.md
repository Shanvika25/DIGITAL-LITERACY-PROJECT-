## Stay Safe Online Checklist
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, ATM PIN, or banking password with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID and name carefully before sending money.
Do not scan unknown QR codes or click suspicious payment links sent through SMS, WhatsApp, or email.
Use strong and unique passwords for your email, banking apps, and social media accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important apps and email accounts.
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking, shopping, or making UPI payments.
Keep your phone, banking apps, and antivirus software updated with the latest security updates.
Never install unknown apps or APK files from unofficial websites.
Be cautious of urgent messages asking for KYC updates, prize claims, or account verification.
Report cyber fraud immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call the National Helpline 1930.
